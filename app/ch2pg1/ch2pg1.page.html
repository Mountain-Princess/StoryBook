Breve

The breve is my most favorite blend of dairy and espresso. It's a creamy, smooth, low carb, high fat deliciousness. In the past I enjoyed too much sugary creamer in my espresso. I've grown up and so have my taste buds, and now I don't need any sugar at all, in fact I don't care for it, and I only take 2t of cream.
